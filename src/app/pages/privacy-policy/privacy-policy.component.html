Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the 4 Bits Threat Detection System. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our threat detection services and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

Our threat detection system may collect and process the following data:

  • Video footage from security cameras
  • Images of detected threats
  • Time and location of detection events
  • System performance metrics
  • User account information (for authorized system administrators)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the purpose of:

  • Identifying and alerting about potential security threats
  • Maintaining system performance and reliability
  • Improving the threat detection algorithms
  • Generating reports and analytics for security management

4. Data Retention

Detection events and related data are typically stored for 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted unless:

  • They are explicitly marked for longer retention by authorized personnel
  • They are part of an ongoing security investigation
  • Retention is required by applicable law or regulation
